About Us

About Golden Kiss

Golden Kiss Private Limited is a proudly Zimbabwean-owned security company founded in 2016 by Mr. Thembinkosi Sibanda. Headquartered in Bulawayo, we specialize in delivering reliable, professional, and innovative security solutions throughout Zimbabwe. We are trusted by individuals, businesses, institutions, and government entities to protect what matters most — with integrity, discipline, and excellence.

Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for consistent quality, rapid response, and customer-focused service. Whether it's securing private residences, commercial properties, construction sites, or special events, Golden Kiss is your dependable partner in safety and protection.

Our Mission

To provide professional, efficient, and reliable security services that ensure peace of mind, protect lives and property, and contribute to the safety and development of Zimbabwean communities.

Our Vision

To become Zimbabwe's most trusted and innovative security service provider, known for delivering peace of mind through excellence and integrity.
